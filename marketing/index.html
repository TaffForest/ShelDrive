How it works

Three steps. Under a minute.

1

Install & mount

Download the app, click Mount. A folder appears on your Mac — just like a USB drive.

2

Drag your files in

Copy files into the folder. They're encrypted on your device and uploaded to the Shelby network automatically.

3

Access anywhere

Your files are stored on decentralised infrastructure. No single company controls them. No monthly fees.

Why ShelDrive?

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Just a folder

Shows up in Finder like any drive. Drag and drop. No special tools, no CLI, no learning curve.

Encrypted by default

Every file is encrypted with AES-256 on your device before it leaves. Only you can read your data.

Works offline

Files are cached locally. Read and write without internet. Changes sync when you're back online.

No subscriptions

Pay once for storage on the Shelby network. No recurring fees. No surprise charges. Your data, your terms.

Content safety

Built-in protection blocks dangerous file types and scans images before they reach the network.

Open source

Every line of code is public. Audit the encryption, verify the storage, trust the process.
